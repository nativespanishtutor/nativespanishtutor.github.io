Top 10 reasons to learn Spanish

  1. Speaking a second language broaden your view of the world.
  2. You develop more self-awareness of your first language and culture.
  3. There are over 450 million native Spanish speakers across the globe.
  4. Spanish is the official language in 21 countries.
  5. You can travel more confidently, make new friends and experience local culture.
  6. Being bilingual is good for your brain; it helps with multi-tasking attention, and memory.
  7. Speaking Spanish makes your resume more competitive.
  8. You can enjoy reading books, listening to music, and watching movies in Spanish.
  9. Bilingual people tend to be more sympathetic with high-functioning emotional skills.
  10. Spanish is considered an accessible language to learners.

Why not start today?
